## About Tigerbotics

Tigerbotics is a high school [FIRST Robotics Competition](https://spectrum3847.org/about-first/) team from [Glen Rose High School](http://www.grisd.net) located in Glen Rose, Texas.
Tigerbotics introduces students to STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Math) through our motto, **"building bots, engineering futures."**

## About This Website

This website serves as a collection point for all information about Tigerbotics, whether it be our cooperative technical documentation base, our [Open Alliance](https://www.theopenalliance.com/) build logs, or simply pictures of our robot, you can find it here.
